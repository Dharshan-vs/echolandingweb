Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ECHO

How does ECHO work?

ECHO uses a scientifically designed hearing test with different sound frequencies and volumes to measure your hearing ability accurately. Our AI algorithms analyze your responses and provide professional-grade results in seconds.

Do I need special headphones?

While you can take the test with regular headphones, for the most accurate results we recommend using high-quality, over-ear headphones. Our smart calibration system adapts to your specific headphone type.

Can I trust the results?

Yes! ECHO uses proven audiology methods and AI-powered analysis, but keep in mind it's not a replacement for a professional audiologist examination. We recommend regular professional check-ups.

Is ECHO free to use?

Yes, ECHO is completely free for all users. We believe hearing health should be accessible to everyone, which is why we've made our core features available at no cost.

How often should I test my hearing?

We recommend taking the test every 612 months, or whenever you feel changes in your hearing. Regular monitoring helps track your hearing health journey and detect any early changes.

What makes ECHO different from other apps?

ECHO combines AI-powered accuracy with gamified testing, making hearing health monitoring engaging and accessible. Our clinical-grade algorithms and user-friendly interface set us apart from traditional hearing test apps.
