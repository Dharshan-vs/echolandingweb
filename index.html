About ECHO

ECHO is an AI-powered mobile hearing test application that helps users monitor their hearing health from the comfort of their homes. Developed by a passionate student team from PSNA College and backed by T-Hub, we're revolutionizing accessible healthcare through innovative technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ECHO

How does ECHO work?

ECHO uses a scientifically designed hearing test with different sound frequencies and volumes to measure your hearing ability accurately.

Do I need special headphones?

While you can take the test with regular headphones, for the most accurate results we recommend using high-quality, over-ear headphones.

Can I trust the results?

Yes! ECHO uses proven audiology methods, but keep in mind it's not a replacement for a professional audiologist examination.

Is ECHO free to use?

Yes, ECHO is completely free for all users.

How often should I test my hearing?

We recommend taking the test every 6–12 months, or whenever you feel changes in your hearing.
